Virat Kohli
Details

Virat Kohli (born November 5, 1988) is an Indian international cricketer and widely regarded as one of the greatest all-format batsmen in the history of the sport. He holds the record for the most centuries in One Day Internationals (ODIs) and the highest run-scorer in the Indian Premier League (IPL).
